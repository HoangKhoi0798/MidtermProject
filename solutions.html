Renewable Energy Prediction and Communication Rejuvenation

Renewable energy is energy derived from natural sources that are replenished at a higher rate than they are consumed. Sunlight and wind, for example, are such sources that are constantly being replenished. Renewable energy sources are plentiful and all around us. Generating renewable energy creates far lower emissions than burning fossil fuelsand generate three times more jobs than fossil fuels.
By utilizing and improving the technology to harvest these kinds of energy, we can:

  • Allow for a wider range of energy sources
  • Reduce the cost energy usage, both on society and climate
  • Avoiding the exhaustion of our planet resources

Despite the efforts in reducing the effects of climate changes, the need for good forecast and communication systems is still important.
Not everything happen on our planet can or should be intervened by human technology, that is why forecasting is important to help people alleviate the effects of natural disaster, as well as the needed communication technology to do so.

Much of the climate changes happening are due to the damage human society have done to nature.
By restoring what was lost, we can improve the condition of our planet and reduce the harmful effects they have on people.
